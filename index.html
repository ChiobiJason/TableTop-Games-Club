TABLETOP GAMES

CLUB

Club Description

The “Tabletop Games Club” strives to connect students through various tabletop games, from Dungeons and Dragons to chess. If you are interested in board games, card games, or even just want a place to hang out, this club is for you.

Club Mission

Our club intends to be an open space for students to let off steam while still being able to focus on schoolwork without the stressful commitment or responsibilities that other clubs may have. We employ a broad topic so that many interests can be served to some degree, ensuring that diversity is encouraged, and our focus on social interaction between students can be preserved.
